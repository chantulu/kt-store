What we are all about

KT’s is an endeavour to make your shopping experience a complete and enjoyable one.
This new age store is a sustainable convenience store and eatery where you can stop in, chat, and
grab a few things—it’s stocked with better-for-you products that are better for the planet too.
With quaint lighting and brick walls, KT’s has a super inviting vibe.
You can walk in, sip on some healthy protein shakes, shop, grab a delicious meal and be a multitasking ninja.


An interesting curation of Organic products, ranging from finger licking jams/chutneys to lentils,
basic conveniences, ‘chakki’ products, gift items and on the go food. KT’s will definitely sort out
your shopping needs. Our food is extremely flavourful and healthy. It ranges from butter chicken
&amp; rice combos to scrumptious sandwiches.
Stop by for a complete shopping experience!
